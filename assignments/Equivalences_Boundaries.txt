Equivalences and Boundaries

- Teacher, Student, TA, and superclass User all have equivalent:
    • sign_in (meaning to log in to the app) operations
    • user-identifying data: email, name, password
- Student and TA have equivalent sign_into_session, session info gathering, course membership info gathering, course enrolling and course leaving operations.
- Teacher and TA both have equivalent get_attendance_records, open session, close session and session info gathering operations.
